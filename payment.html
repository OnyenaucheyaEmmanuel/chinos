Payment Support

Structured help for school fees, rent, medical and other key payments that must not fail.

Use cases we support

  • School fees with proper admission and invoice documents.
  • Residential or commercial rent backed by agreements.
  • Medical bills from recognized hospitals.
  • Verified vendor or project invoices.
Our approach

We validate the obligation, align on a repayment plan and, where suitable, settle directly to the beneficiary. You then meet your commitments to Chinos Finance under a documented schedule.

What we expect from you
  • Genuine documents and accurate information.
  • Verifiable identity and income.
  • Early communication if circumstances change.
